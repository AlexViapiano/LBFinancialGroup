About Us

Latella & Bastone Financial Group provides our clients with a holistic approach to managing their financial well-being. Through strategic partnerships with Bastone & Associates Inc. and Manulife Securities Inc., our clients benefit from leading industry experience and knowledge. Comprised primarily of business owners and professionals, they receive timely expertise through a comprehensive service offering that includes: Dedicated tax planning, advanced insurance concepts and a leading industry investment management team. Positioning itself as a niche in the financial service industry, Latella & Bastone Financial Group aim to foster close relationships with our clients that are built on trust and professionalism.
